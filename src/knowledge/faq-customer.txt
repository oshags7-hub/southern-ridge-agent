================================================================================

Q: Do you sell raw milk?
A: We offer raw milk through our herdshare program. Under Tennessee law, raw milk
   cannot be sold retail — but you can own a share of our herd and receive your
   portion of their milk production. Contact us to learn more about herdshare membership.

A: A herdshare means you own a percentage of our dairy animals. We board and care for
   your animals on the farm. In return, you receive your share of their milk each week.
