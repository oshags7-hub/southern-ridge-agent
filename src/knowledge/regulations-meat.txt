USDA FSIS REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
Federal Meat Inspection Act applies to interstate commerce.
Intrastate (Tennessee-only) sales may qualify for exemption under:
  - Retail exemption (selling directly to end consumer)
  - Custom exempt (not for resale, uninspected)
  - State-inspected facilities (TDA-inspected processing)

Important: Animals must be slaughtered and processed at a USDA-inspected
or TDA-inspected facility unless qualifying for a specific exemption.

For custom-exempt processing (personal use, not resale):
  - Animal must be owned by purchaser prior to slaughter
  - Cannot be sold in commerce

================================================================================
HACCP REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
HACCP (Hazard Analysis Critical Control Points) plans required for:
  - Any further processing beyond whole cuts (grinding, smoking, curing)
  - Inspected facilities handling product for resale

House-made sausage:
  - Requires processing at inspected facility with HACCP plan, OR
  - Falls under retail exemption if made and sold on-premises to end consumer
  - Confirm current compliance status with processor and TDA

Smoked/cured products (brisket, smoked items):
  - Smoking for sale requires time/temp documentation
  - Confirm with processor whether this is covered under their HACCP plan

================================================================================
LABELING REQUIREMENTS
================================================================================
Required on all retail meat packages:
  - Product name (common or usual name)
  - Net weight
  - Ingredients (if not a single-ingredient product)
  - Inspection legend or retail exemption statement
  - Handler/packer name and address
  - Safe handling instructions (for raw products)

For house-made sausage specifically:
  - Ingredient statement required (pork, spices — list all)
  - Allergen statement if applicable
  - "Keep refrigerated" or "Keep frozen" storage instruction
